# Short Variant — 3 Landing Ideas

## Idea A — One-Screen Decision
- Screen 1: strong H1 + 3 value bullets + mini 6-level infographic + primary CTA.
- Screen 2: compact "Jak to działa w 3 krokach" block.
- Screen 3: form + short trust-strip (eIDAS / RODO / 2 400+).
- Target: ad traffic needing fast conversion with minimal scroll noise.

## Idea B — Problem → Proof → Form
- Screen 1: offer + 2 buttons (Dołącz / Jak to działa).
- Screen 2: 4 key rental risks + inline "jak Rent Standard je neutralizuje".
- Screen 3: form + micro-FAQ (3 questions).
- Target: rational audience needing cause-effect argumentation before the form.

## Idea C — Legal Confidence (selected)
- Screen 1: "Ekspercka ochrona najmu" + short sub-headline + 5 polished Polish theses.
- Screen 2: 3 cards "Co kupujesz": Umowa ekspercka / Podpis + weryfikacja / Mediacja + wsparcie prawne.
- Screen 3: form (unchanged business logic) + hard legal trust markers.
- Target: audience 40–60, focus on legal reliability and control.

## Why Idea C was selected
- Best alignment with target audience (property owners 40–60 who value legal seriousness).
- Preserves service essence without scroll fatigue.
- 5 theses already validated in earlier copy iteration.
